## Story 1: Building From Nothing — $75M Startup Tax Product
**Competency:** Initiative, building from scratch, cross-functional collaboration

**S:** Fieldguide, a $75M-funded AI audit platform, had zero tax practitioners on staff. Their tax module was a trial balance viewer with no engagement workflow, no demo, and a January 2026 launch date.
**T:** As sole tax SME (contract via Siegfried), deliver 6 defined objectives to take the tax product from zero to market-ready.
**A:** Created end-to-end 1120 and 1065 engagement templates. Built a 40+ item competitive validation matrix vs CaseWare, Thomson Reuters, and CCH. Defined AI use cases for tax. Created the Tax Golden Demo. Built a pilot playbook. Trained the entire product team via Loom videos and daily standups.
**R:** All 6 objectives delivered on time. Platform went from TB-only viewer to demonstrable E2E tax engagement in under 8 weeks. Fieldguide had a launch-ready product, trained team, and repeatable sales playbook.

## Story 2: Leadership / Growth — Fortune 5 Provision Audit (Walmart)
**Competency:** Technical depth, progressive responsibility, leadership

**S:** At EY, assigned to the ASC 740 audit for Walmart — a Fortune 5 global retailer with 500+ holding companies across 20+ international jurisdictions.
**T:** Progress from staff-level work on deferred items to managing the day-to-day audit of the entire income tax provision.
**A:** Started working on deferred and fixed asset deferreds, expanded to current items, FIN 48/UTP, and ultimately managed all aspects of the provision audit. Oversaw 3+ professionals. Created testing procedures for hundreds of global entities. Directed the UTP workstream across 20+ jurisdictions.
**R:** Progressed from Staff to acting Manager-level responsibility over 4 years. Audit delivered on time every year. Built the institutional knowledge that the engagement relied on.

Managed engagement economics — resource scheduling, budget monitoring, client billing. Served as primary client contact for the family office.

## Story 4: Automation / Efficiency — $10K+ Workpaper Automation
**Competency:** Process improvement, technology adoption, ROI delivery

**S:** At EY, recurring tax processes (cash forecasting, state withholding, compliance workpapers) consumed significant manual effort each quarter.
**T:** Reduce preparation time and manual effort across multiple recurring processes.
**A:** Engineered automated workpaper solutions using Power Query to streamline quarterly cash tax forecasts — transforming raw ERP data into dynamic, audit-ready models. Built "Evergreen" templates synchronizing data across multi-quarter periods. Optimized state withholding calculation templates.
**R:** 90% reduction in cash tax forecast preparation time. 8+ hours saved per quarter on state withholding. $10,000+ annual net profit savings from workpaper automation. Templates adopted as standard methodology.

## Story 7: AI / Innovation — Tax AI Use Cases at Fieldguide

**T:** Define and validate AI use cases that would differentiate Fieldguide's tax product.
**A:** Created an AI Prompts Document with tested examples. Defined use cases: TB-to-M-1 identification, self-review checklists, tax code suggestion, document analysis. **R:** Fieldguide could market AI-powered tax workflows as a competitive differentiator no legacy tool offers. Engineering had validated, practitioner-approved specs to build against. Foundation laid for an AI review agent that catches errors before human review.

## Story 14: Data Integration — Rosetta Stone of Tax Data
**Competency:** Systems thinking, technical mapping, product strategy

**S:** At Fieldguide, no framework existed to map tax codes across different software platforms — each system (CCH, UltraTax, Lacerte, Thomson Reuters) used different codes and structures.
**T:** Create a universal mapping framework that would allow Fieldguide's platform to import/export data across all major tax software.
**A:** Built tax code mapping frameworks for CCH, UltraTax, Lacerte, and Reuters. Created end-to-end process maps for 1120 and 1065 workflows. Documented M-3 codes and tax coding frameworks. Tested and validated every workflow path.
**R:** Platform could credibly claim interoperability with all major tax software. Universal mapping became the foundation for data import/export functionality. Sales could demo cross-platform capabilities to prospects.
