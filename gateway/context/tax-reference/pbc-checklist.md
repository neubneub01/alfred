# Standard PBC (Provided by Client) Request List

## Financial Data
1. **Trial Balance** — Year-end adjusted trial balance (Excel format preferred)
2. **General Ledger Detail** — Full GL detail for the fiscal year
3. **Financial Statements** — Audited/reviewed financial statements (current + prior year)
4. **Consolidating Schedules** — If multi-entity, consolidating/eliminating entries

## Tax Returns & Filings
5. **Prior Year Federal Return** — Form 1120/1120-S/1065 and all schedules
6. **Prior Year State Returns** — All state income/franchise tax returns filed
7. **Extension Filings** — Form 7004 and state extensions
8. **Estimated Tax Payments** — Federal and state estimated payments made

## Fixed Assets & Depreciation
9. **Fixed Asset Register** — Detailed listing with dates, costs, methods, lives
10. **Asset Additions/Dispositions** — Current year additions, retirements, sales
11. **Tax Depreciation Schedules** — MACRS schedules from prior year return

## Compensation & Benefits
12. **Officer Compensation** — Detail of all officer/shareholder compensation
13. **Stock Compensation** — Grants, exercises, vestings (ISOs and NSOs)
14. **Retirement Plan Contributions** — 401(k), pension, profit sharing details
15. **Bonus Accruals** — Year-end bonus accruals and payment dates (2½ month rule)

## Entity Structure
16. **Org Chart** — Legal entity organizational chart
17. **Ownership Changes** — Any changes in ownership during the year
18. **Intercompany Transactions** — Loans, charges, transfers between related entities
19. **New Entities** — Any entities formed, acquired, or dissolved

## Specific Items
20. **Lease Schedules** — ASC 842 lease schedules (ROU assets/liabilities)
21. **Bad Debt / AR Reserve** — Aged receivables and reserve analysis
22. **Inventory Analysis** — Inventory valuation, LIFO/FIFO, reserves
23. **Prepaid / Accrued Expenses** — Detailed breakdowns
24. **R&D Expenditures** — Qualified research expenses for Section 41 credit
25. **Charitable Contributions** — Donations with receipts

## State & Multi-Jurisdiction
26. **State Apportionment Data** — Revenue, payroll, property by state
27. **Nexus Questionnaire** — States with physical/economic presence
28. **State Tax Credits** — Available state credits and carryforwards

## ASC 740 Specific
29. **Tax Provision Workpapers** — Prior year provision (current + deferred)
30. **Deferred Tax Roll-Forward** — Opening → additions → reversals → closing
31. **Uncertain Tax Positions** — FIN 48 / ASC 740-10 reserve schedule
32. **Rate Reconciliation** — Prior year statutory-to-effective rate reconciliation
33. **Valuation Allowance Analysis** — Support for VA position (positive/negative evidence)

## Other
34. **Tax Notices** — Any IRS/state correspondence or audit activity
35. **Transfer Pricing Documentation** — If applicable (Section 482)
36. **Foreign Operations** — Forms 5471, 8865, 8858 if applicable
