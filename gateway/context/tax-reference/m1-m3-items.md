# Common M-1 / M-3 Book-Tax Differences

## Schedule M-1 (Form 1120)
Reconciliation of income per books with income per return.

## Schedule M-3 (Form 1120)
Required when total assets ≥ $10M. More detailed breakdown by temporary vs. permanent.

## Permanent Differences

These never reverse — they permanently differ between book and tax.

| Item | Book Treatment | Tax Treatment | M-1/M-3 Adjustment |
|------|---------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Meals & entertainment | Expense | 50% deductible (meals), 0% (entertainment) | Add back 50-100% |
| Fines & penalties | Expense | Non-deductible | Add back |
| Life insurance premiums (key person) | Expense | Non-deductible | Add back |
| Life insurance proceeds | Income | Tax-exempt | Subtract |
| Municipal bond interest | Income | Tax-exempt | Subtract |
| Domestic production activities | — | Deduction (Section 199A) | Subtract |
| Stock compensation (ISO) | Expense | No deduction until disposition | Add back (timing varies) |
| Political contributions | Expense | Non-deductible | Add back |
| Federal income tax expense | Expense | Non-deductible | Add back |
| Goodwill impairment (non-deductible) | Expense | Non-deductible | Add back |

## Temporary Differences

These reverse over time — create deferred tax assets or liabilities.

| Item | Book Treatment | Tax Treatment | DTA or DTL |
|------|---------------|---------------|------------|
| Depreciation | Straight-line | MACRS / Bonus | DTL (tax faster) |
| Bad debt reserve | Allowance method | Direct write-off | DTA |
| Warranty reserve | Accrued when sold | Deductible when paid | DTA |
| Prepaid revenue | Deferred on books | Taxable when received | DTL |
| Accrued bonuses | Accrued when earned | Deductible when paid (2½ month rule) | DTA |
| Vacation accrual | Accrued when earned | Deductible when paid | DTA |
| Inventory reserves | GAAP reserves | Not deductible until disposed | DTA |
| ASC 842 lease adjustments | ROU asset/liability | Cash basis | DTA/DTL |
| Section 174 R&D | Expense | Capitalize & amortize (5/15 yr) | DTL |
| NOL carryforward | N/A | Carryforward (80% limit post-TCJA) | DTA |

## Key Tax Reform Items (TCJA / Post-TCJA)

- **Section 174**: R&D expenses must be capitalized and amortized (5 years domestic, 15 years foreign) — effective for tax years beginning after 12/31/2021
- **Section 163(j)**: Business interest deduction limited to 30% of adjusted taxable income
- **NOL limitations**: Post-2017 NOLs limited to 80% of taxable income, indefinite carryforward
- **GILTI**: Global Intangible Low-Taxed Income inclusion for CFCs
- **BEAT**: Base Erosion and Anti-Abuse Tax for large multinationals
- **Bonus depreciation**: 100% for assets placed in service 2017-2022, phasing down 20%/year through 2026 (60% in 2024, 40% in 2025, 20% in 2026)
